Upani Perera

Author | Editor

Upani Perera is a Sri Lankan-born, Melbourne-raised writer and editor. She has achieved an Associates Degree in Professional Writing and Editing and is studying a Bachelor's Degree in Creative Writing, both at RMIT. Upani writes contemporary short stories about life, lesbians and love.

Upani currently lives on unceded Bunurong land, and pays respects to the traditional owners and their elders past, present, and future.
